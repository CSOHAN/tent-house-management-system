# Tent House Management System (THMS)

## 📌 Overview

Tent House Management System (THMS) is an enterprise-grade backend application built to digitize and automate the operations of a tent house rental business.

The system manages customers, bookings, inventory, payments, employees, vehicles, and reporting while preventing operational issues such as double bookings and inventory shortages.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Customer Management
- Booking Management
- Inventory Management
- Payment Tracking
- Employee Management
- Vehicle Management
- Reports & Analytics
- AI-assisted Operations
